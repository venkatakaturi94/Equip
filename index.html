Sole Proprietor Information
Sole Proprietor Information

Equip Training Solutions is a sole proprietor concern of Mrs. Gayatri.

Mrs. Gayatri, through Equip Training Solutions, is a training, learning and development consultant and facilitator, holding a Masters Degree in HRM and a Bachelors degree in Education. She has held various positions with several small and large corporations in the fields of Behavioural Training, Coaching and Leadership Development.

With a strong foot in Training, learning and development, Mrs. Gayatri strongly believes in transformation through skills and knowledge should be accessible to all. This belief gave shape to Equip Training Solutions. The last decade has been an eventful time!

Equip is specialised in the gamut of Soft Skills, Aptitude Skills, Technical Training in UG and PG colleges, with deep expertise across delivery mechanisms – Class room Learning, Blended Learning, and Social Learning.

“Train” the students to “Transform” and “Transpose” to the Industry prerequisite, stands as a back bone to the service delivery at Equip.

After years of operating successfully across major cities in Karnataka, AP, Telangana and Odisha, through a strong network of Regional Relationship Managers and Training fraternity, Equip Training Solution is among the best Indian training companies.
